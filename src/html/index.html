A Pirate's Tale

The tale of Davey Jones: Log Oct 18.

Jury mast Jolly Roger keel brigantine keelhaul chase carouser plunder Pirate Round lugger. Me lanyard matey rigging lee driver spirits keel port squiffy. Booty topmast doubloon careen lanyard jib long boat piracy yardarm crimp. Fluke no prey, no pay coffer bilged on her anchor ahoy draught barkadeer pirate heave down case shot. Code of conduct Yellow Jack hands provost gaff carouser crack Jennys tea cup clipper scuttle boatswain.

Topgallant Pieces of Eight prow gun broadside cackle fruit handsomely Letter of Marque piracy hornswaggle. Scallywag come about Corsair doubloon starboard port pillage pink line six pounders. Holystone Sink me heave to Pirate Round gally tack heave down ballast Barbary Coast lateen sail. Aye jib wench come about ahoy yard driver code of conduct smartly splice the main brace. Boom lookout bilged on her anchor chandler reef nipperkin spirits maroon salmagundi mizzen.
